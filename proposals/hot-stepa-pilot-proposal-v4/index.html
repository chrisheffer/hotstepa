Break-even signal

What CPL means

CPL means cost per lead. The first-stage test is whether Meta can create qualified leads at the right cost. Lead-to-sign-up conversion then shows whether the lead quality or signup process needs work.

First decision

Three possible outcomes

Make the conclusion visually obvious: this pilot exists to create a confident next decision.

01

Scale

CPL is low, lead volume is strong, and downstream data shows enough evidence that the leads can become completed partner sign-ups.

02

Refine

CPL is promising, but leads are not converting into sign-ups clearly enough. The next step is to diagnose whether the leads are the wrong fit or the signup process is creating too much friction.

03

Stop

CPL is too high, lead quality is weak, and the ad signal does not justify spending more before the offer or audience is reworked.
