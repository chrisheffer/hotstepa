First decision

Three possible outcomes

Make the conclusion visually obvious: this pilot exists to create a confident next decision.

01

Scale

CPL is low, lead volume is strong, and downstream data shows early evidence that leads can become completed partner sign-ups.

02

Refine

CPL is promising, but conversion data is incomplete, tracking needs work, or only some hooks and audiences are performing.

03

Stop

CPL is too high, lead quality is weak, and there is no credible route to profitable acquisition from the first signal.
